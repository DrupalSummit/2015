About the Summit

Since 2009, the Pacific NW Drupal Summit has been the premier regional event for Drupal professionals in the Pacific Northwest. More than 200 Drupal professionals have attended and enjoyed our two-day event each of the last six years.The event rotates yearly between Seattle WA, Portland OR and Vancouver BC.

The 2015 Pacific Northwest Drupal Summit will be held at Mary Gates Hall on the University of Washington Campus in Seattle.
