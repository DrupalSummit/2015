Conference Overview - PNW Drupal Summit  - Seattle - October 10-11, 2015

Since 2009, the PNW Drupal Summit is the premier regional event for Drupal professionals in the Pacific Northwest. More than 200 Drupal professional have attended and enjoyed our two-day events for the previous six years (2009-2014). The Summit moves each year between Vancouver, BC; Seattle, WA; and Portland, OR; where the local Drupal community organizes the event with volunteers.

Registration is $55 per ticket, starts on front page, and links to a registration form. Drupal Association processes payments. Submit ticket questions on contact form.

To plan your arrival and departure, see Schedule Overview and Transportation. 

Book your hotel room early at Hotel Deca while rooms are available.

This year's Summit venue will be at the University Of Washington, Mary Gates Hall, in Seattle on Saturday and Sunday, October 10th and 11th.

There will be two organized social events, Oct 9th Friday night at Hotel Decca and Oct 10th Saturday night at The Garage.

Session Schedule is pending review and approval of proposed sessions.

The conference will provide lunch and refreshments on Saturday and Sunday at the venue so you can enjoy your lunch with other attendees.

To volunteer for the 2015 Summit, submit the Volunteer sign up form.

If you have time to explore Seattle, check out Dining and Attractions.

Funding for the Summit is provided by attendee registration fees and by our Sponsors. All money raised goes toward venue fees, food & refreshments, hotel coder lounge, Friday night and Saturday night social events, swag, and miscellaneous expenses. Surplus money is forwarded to the next year's Summit account.

Sprints are being organized via DETAILS/CODE SPRINTS

BoFs will be organized during the Summit by the participants.

Attendees are expected to adhere to the conference Code of Conduct.

The 2014 and 2015 Summit web sites are hosted by Acquia. (The 2014 site is temporarily out-of-service.)

If you cannot find what you need on this web site, use the Contact Form.
