Be a Sponsor!

The Pacific Northwest Drupal Summit is an annual event for Drupal professionals to gather, exchange ideas, learn from one another, and advance Drupal. Come expand your coding skills, and enjoy some face-to-face time with other Drupal professionals.

The event will be held in the same location as the 2012 Summit and be held on October 9-11th. This year we're expecting to sell out and attract 350 of the area's best.

We have an enthusiastic, experienced and focused entirely volunteer group backed with the assistance of the Drupal Association to ensure that this event comes off even better than the last sold-out Seattle-based PNWDS.  All we need now is your sponsorship to make it happen.

Contact sponsor@pnwdrupalsummit.org to reserve a sponsorship early. Remember to buy your tickets early as tickets to this event always sell out, too!
